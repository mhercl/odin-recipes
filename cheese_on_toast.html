Cheese on toast!

Lovelly cheesy cheese on toast, eat when sober, or drunk.

Ingredients

  • Cheese!, mature chedder is the best cheese for this
  • Bread! Thick cut bloomer
  • Butter
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Grate the cheese
  2. Butter the bread
  3. Put the grated cheese on the bread
  4. Put it under a broiler
  5. Wait until the cheese has melted and is just starting to brown
  6. Plate up
  7. Add some pepper
  8. ENJOY!!
