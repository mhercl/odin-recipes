How to fry a steak

This is how a steak is cooked in a fancy restaurant

Ingredients

  • Steak! Preferably a ribeye
  • Some kind of cooking oil
  • Garlic,2 crushed and peeled cloves of
  • Tyme, fesh if possible
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Let the steak come up to room temp
  2. Put some oil into a frying pan
  3. Get the pan nice and hot
  4. Generously season both sides of the steak with salt and pepper
  5. Fry the steak for about 3ish minuets each side

    This may help you decide if it's done to your liking

  6. Put the butter, garlic and Tyme in the pan for the last minuet of the frying time and baste the steak
  7. When done, let the steak rest
  8. ENJOY!!
