Scrambled Eggs!

Scrambled eggs to die for!!

These eggs are amazing!! I modified Gordon Ramseys recepie to make what I would consider to be a superior plate of eggy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • A large knob of butter
  • A pinch of MSG
  • A pinch of salt
  • A good pinch of Pepper

Steps

  1. Carfully crack the eggs into a bowl and beat with a fork untill no curds remain.
  2. Add the MSG to the beaten eggs
  3. Put the butter in a non-stick saucepan and heat on full until the butter starts to brown.
  4. Add the eggs to the pan.
  5. leave the eggs for 20 seconds, I use this time to tidy up any mess I have made.
  6. After 20 seconds take the pan off the heat and using a plastic spatula, scrape the cooked eggs of the pan and scramble them!!
  7. Repeat the previous steps until your eggs are just about to your liking, they will cook for a bit after you take them off the heat.
  8. Serve on a plate and sprinkle the salt and pepper.
  9. ENJOY!!
